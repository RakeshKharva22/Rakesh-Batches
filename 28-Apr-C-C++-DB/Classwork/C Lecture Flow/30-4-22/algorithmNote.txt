
Algorithm :- We write down steps for a particular process.

e.g Making a Tea

Step 1 :  Start
Step 2 : Input (Milk,water,sugar,etc)
Step 3 : Processing ( Boiling)
Step 4 : Decide( If Tea Good goto step 5, if not goto step1
Step 5 : Output (Serve)
Step 6 : Stop.
 
2nd Process Addition of 2 No.s

Step 1: Start
Step 2: Input (a=10 , b=20)
Step 3 : Process (c = a + b)
Step 4 : If No.+ve goto step 5,if -ve goto step 2;
Step 5 : Output (c)
Step 6 : Stop
